Section 2 – Psychological & Motivation Questions
Not for applications
The following questions serve to assess the motivation, teamwork and psychological stability of the soldier.
Note: Not all questions need to be answered.
1. Personality & Background
How would you describe your most important character traits?
Have there been events in your life that have shaped you?
What long-term goals do you pursue in your life?
2. Emotional Stability
How do you react when you suddenly come under strong pressure?
Do you have difficulty calming down when you are stressed?
Do you have nightmares or sleep disorders?
Can you consciously control strong negative feelings?
3. Aggression Control
How do you deal with situations that make you angry?
Have you ever lost control of your behavior?
Can you stay calm in conflicts, even when provoked?
What was the most difficult situation in which you had to control your aggression?
How do you react to disrespect or insults?
4. Teamwork & Discipline
What role do you usually take on in a team?
Can you accept decisions even if you disagree?
Do you have problems with authority figures?
How would you describe your sense of responsibility?
5. Morality & Decision Making
Where do you draw your personal moral boundaries in deployment?
Can you make tough decisions when the situation requires it?
How do you deal with feelings of guilt after difficult decisions?
What is more important to you: the mission or your conscience?
6. Mental Health
Have you ever sought psychological help?
Have you noticed any changes in your mood or thinking lately?
Do you suffer from anxiety, panic or depressive phases?
Do you use substances to cope with stress?
Have/had you negative thoughts about others or yourself?
Section 3 – Medical Assessment
1. General Health Condition
Do you take medication regularly?
Have you noticed any weight loss/gain recently?
2. Injury History
Have you suffered fractures, surgeries or serious injuries in the past?
Have you ever fainted or experienced loss of consciousness?
Do you have problems with balance, dizziness or coordination disorders?
3. Substances & Dependencies
Do you consume alcohol or other psychoactive substances?
Have you ever been treated for drug or alcohol abuse?
Are there known addiction diseases in your family?
Section 4 – Fitness Test (execute at sports field)
1. 15x Push-Ups (Result)
2. 10x Sit-Ups (Result)
3. 10x Burpee (Result)
4. 2 rounds running (Time in minutes)
Note: This test should be conducted under the supervision of a qualified instructor. The results serve to assess physical fitness for service.
Section 5 – Situational Questions for Deployments
Not for applications
These questions serve for self-reflection and assessment of satisfaction with previous service time.
1. Were you satisfied with the last deployments?
2. Are there certain situations in service that particularly bother you?
3. Self-reflection of the deployment/normal unit work.
